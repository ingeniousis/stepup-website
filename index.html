Frequently Asked Questions

How does step tracking work with my phone?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in fitness tracker - Google Fit on Android and the motion tracker on iPhones. Your phone counts your steps from your pocket, hand or bag in the background without impacting battery.
No need for a fitness band! But you can sync steps from a fitness band via Apple Health or Google Fit if you'd like.
Do you support wearables like Apple Watch, Jawbone, Misfit, Xiomi, Garmin etc?
StepUp works with just your phone. And you can also sync steps from supported fitness bands to StepUp via Apple Health or Google Fit.

iPhone/Apple Health supported devices: Apple Watch, Jawbone, Garmin, Misfit, Pebble, Xiaomi Withings and many more.
NOTE: You need to sync steps from your device to Apple Health via it's app, and you need to sync StepUp with Apple Health via StepUp settings > Sync wearable.

Android/Google Fit supported devices: Android Wear devices, Xiaomi, Moto 360, Garmin, Withings and many more. NOTE: You need to sync steps from your device to Google Fit and it will automatically sync with StepUp.
How do I add friends in StepUp?
Simply log in with Facebook in StepUp, and you will be able to see your Facebook friends who are also on StepUp.
You can invite your friends to get StepUp, and you will see them in the app once they download it and sign in with Facebook.
NOTE: You can control which friends you see (and who can see you) in StepUp.
PS: We NEVER post anything on Facebook.
Who can see my steps?
Unless you log in with Facebook on StepUp, only you can see your steps.
When you log in with Facebook in StepUp, you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp, and they will be able to see you in StepUp.
You can see your friends' today steps, yesterday steps & 7-day average steps. Similarly, your friends who're on StepUp can see these for you.
Also, in the friend head to head page, you can see best day steps & best week steps.
NOTE: You can privately hide (block) certain friends on StepUp and then they won't see you in StepUp, and you won't see them.
How can I hide (block) friends in StepUp?
When you log in with Facebook in StepUp, you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp. You can privately hide any of these friends in StepUp - then you won't see hidden friends in StepUp, and they won't see you in StepUp.
NOTE: This is only within the StepUp app, and doesn't affect Facebook in any way. You can "unhide" them at anytime from StepUp settings.
How accurate is the step tracking? (Very accurate!)
StepUp uses your phone's built-in fitness tracker, which counts your steps from your pocket, hand or bag. Studies show that phone activity trackers can be more accurate than wearable activity trackers.
Will StepUp drain my battery? (NOT at all!)
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which works in the background without impacting battery. Apps that use your location drain battery, but since StepUp doesn't use your location, it is very battery friendly! :)
Which phones are supported?
StepUp works on iPhones & Android phones.
iPhones: iPhone 5s and newer (6, 6+, 6s, 6s+, SE, 7, 7+)
Android: All android devices with Kitkat or newer OS version like Samsung, Moto, LG, Nexus, OnePlus, Xiami, Micromax, Karbon etc
