Privacy policy

StepUp's goal is to help users live more active and healthier lives using advanced mobile internet technology. To that end our applications and services collect, store and analyze a variety of activity related data, both on your mobile device and via cloud-based services.
When you use StepUp, we receive some personal information from you like the type of device you’re using and your IP address. You can choose to share additional information, for example to help us monitor the performance of your installed app or to allow our service to send you push notifications from time to time. We give you control through your settings to limit the data we collect from you and how we use it. You can also always download the personal information related to you on StepUp.
This statement is intended to clearly explain in human readable language what data we collect and how it is used. If after reading this statement you have further questions, please contact us contact@thestepupapp.com.

Information collected

When you choose to share the information below with us, we may collect the following types of information in order to provide services:

  • Activity related data: daily step goal, steps taken, floors climbed (iOS only), distance traveled
  • Calorie Profile (optional): age, gender, height, weight (used to calculate calories burnt)
  • Facebook Login (optional): Profile info, friend list, email address
  • Google Login (optional): Name, email address, profile photo
  • Apple Login (optional): Name, email address
  • Your friends on StepUp (optional)
    • None of this data is shared publicly outside of the StepUp app.


    Login credentials

    When creating a StepUp account you can use an identity from Apple, Google or Facebook. StepUp will not have access to any of your login credentials (e.g. username or password) from these login services and will only have access to a token. A token is an identifier created by the login provider for StepUp to use for the purpose of identifying you on their systems.


    Public Information

    The information you provide StepUp is not available publicly. Only individuals with a StepUp account who you explicitly connect with using the StepUp service can see this information.


    Access to Device’s Health Services

    In order to track the steps you take, StepUp uses the Health Services provided by the mobile device on which the StepUp app is installed.
    For iOS you will be asked for permission for us to access your motion data or Apple Health. This provides the StepUp app access to the following information provided by your iOS Device:
    Steps, Distance travelled, floors climbed
    For Android you will be asked for permission to access Google Fit. This provides the StepUp app access to the following information provided by your Android Device:
    Steps, Distance travelled, floors climbed
    This data is not shared publicly but we use it to display to those StepUp users who you have invited to connect to you or those who’s invitation to connect you have accepted.


    Analytics Information

    We use of Google Analytics for Firebase to understand the usage of StepUp in order to improve our product for users. A list of data captured by default by Google Analytics for Firebase can be found here.
    In addition we capture anonymized data to improve app usabiliity about usage events like Install, SignUp, SetDailyGoal, InviteFriend, Cheer, Taunt, Nudge, AddFriend, HideFriend, SeeFriendDetail, SeeHistory, SeeAlerts, EnabledNotifications etc.


    Use of your data

    App usage data

    We use analytics tools to track in-app behavior anonymously for the purpose of informing future product improvements and creating a better StepUp experience. Examples of in-app behavior include frequency of use, user retention, and in-app page views.


    Aggregated user data research

    We may conduct research about our users' behavior and demographics based on the information provided through the use of our services, for the purposes of improving our services. Such research will only compile user data in an aggregated, de-identified statistics. We may share these statistics with affiliates and business partners. This information will NEVER identify you personally.


    Deleting Your Data

    Your basic account information and information from your device’s health services are fundamental to providing our services. You can remove access to these by closing your account and requesting all personal data is erased. You can delete your account via the StepUp app in the settings page. Go to “Send Feedback” and say “delete my data”. Deletion requests will be completed within 30 days of receiving your feedback request. Or email us on contact@thestepupapp.com with the email subject "delete my data" from the email associated with your account.

    If you haven't created an account, you can simply uninstall the app.

    Social sharing

    If you choose to use the social features of StepUp, your daily activity data may be visible to your friends on StepUp. By using this feature of our services you consent to sharing this information. You can choose to stop sharing data with your friends on StepUp at any time by blocking friends that you don't want to share your step data with. You can choose to stop sharing daily step data with all your friends on StepUp by logging out of StepUp.


    Communication

    If you choose to share contact information with us (such as an email address), we may use this information to send you updates about our services from time to time. If this information is promotional in nature, we will provide an opt-out option.


    Policy for children

    StepUp’s services are not directed at persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, we ask that you do not submit any personal data through our services. If you are aware of a person under the age of 13 who has submitted personal information to our services, please report it to us so we can delete this data.


    Changes to this policy

    We reserve the right to change our Privacy Policy in the future without prior notice. The services we offer are likely to change, and in the future it is therefore likely that our Privacy Policy will also need to be revised accordingly. Any changes to our privacy policy will be posted here.


    More information

    Contact us at contact@thestepupapp.com if you have further questions or concerns about your data.
