Frequently Asked Questions

How does step tracking work with my phone?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker. It counts your steps as you as you move with it in your pocket, hand or bag, and works in the background without impacting battery.
How is the step tracking so accurate?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which counts your steps as you as you move with it in your pocket, hand or bag. Some studies show that phone activity trackers are actually more accurate than wearable activity trackers.
How does this work in the background when my app is not opened?
StepUp just uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which works in the background without impacting battery. You don't need to have the app opened all the time for it to count your steps.
How is StepUp it so battery-friendly?
StepUp just uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which works in the background without impacting battery. Apps that use your location drain battery, but since we don't use your location, we are very battery friendly! :)
How do I add friends in StepUp?
All you and your friends need is a smartphone with StepUp. Simply log in with Facebook in StepUp, and you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp.
How can I block (hide) friends in StepUp?
When you log in with Facebook in StepUp, you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp. You can privately block any of these friends in StepUp - then you won't see blocked friends in StepUp, and they won't see you in StepUp.
This is only within the StepUp app, and doesn't affect Facebook in any way. You can unblock them at anytime.
Who can see my steps?
When you log in with Facebook in StepUp, you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp, and they will be able to see you in StepUp.
You can see you friends today's steps, yesterday's steps & 7-day average steps. Similarly, your friends who're on StepUp can see your today's steps, yesterday's steps & 7-day average.
Also, in the friend head to head page, you can see your best day steps & best week steps.
Which phones are supported?
StepUp works on iOS & Android - for all phones that have a step sensor.
iOS: iOS8+ & iPhone 5s, 6, 6+, 6s, 6s+ & iOS 8+
Android: KitKat+ & devices with step sensor like Galaxy, Nexus, Note etc
Why is my phone not supported?
StepUp uses a smartphone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker.
