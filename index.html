Frequently Asked Questions

How does step tracking work with my phone?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor capability. In iOS, it uses the built in motion tracker. In Android, it uses the in-built Google Fit service. In both cases, it counts your steps as you as you move with it in your pocket, hand or bag, and works in the background without impacting battery.
How is the step tracking so accurate?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which counts your steps as you as you move with it in your pocket, hand or bag. Some studies show that phone activity trackers are actually more accurate than wearable activity trackers.
How does this work in the background when my app is not opened?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which works in the background without impacting battery. You don't need to have the app opened all the time for it to count your steps.
How is StepUp so battery-friendly?
StepUp uses your phone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker, which works in the background without impacting battery. Apps that use your location drain battery, but since we don't use your location, we are very battery friendly! :)
How do I add friends in StepUp?
Simply log in with Facebook in StepUp, and you will be able to see your Facebook friends who are also on StepUp. All you and your friends need is a smartphone (iOS/Android) with StepUp.
How can I block (hide) friends in StepUp?
When you log in with Facebook in StepUp, you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp. You can privately block any of these friends in StepUp - then you won't see blocked friends in StepUp, and they won't see you in StepUp.
This is only within the StepUp app, and doesn't affect Facebook in any way. You can unblock them at anytime.
Who can see my steps?
When you log in with Facebook in StepUp, you will be able to see your friends who are also on StepUp, and they will be able to see you in StepUp.
You can see your friends today's steps, yesterday's steps & 7-day average steps. Similarly, your friends who're on StepUp can see your today's steps, yesterday's steps & 7-day average.
Also, in the friend head to head page, you can see best day steps & best week steps.
Which phones are supported?
StepUp works on iOS & Android - for all phones that have a step sensor.
iOS: iOS8+ & iPhone 5s, 6, 6+, 6s, 6s+ & iOS 8+
Android: All android devices with Kitkat or newer OS version like Samsung, Moto, LG, Nexus, OnePlus, Xiami, Micromax, Karbon etc
Why is my phone not supported?
StepUp uses a smartphone's built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker.
Do you support wearables like FitBit, Jawbone, Apple Watch, Microsoft Band?
StepUp currently uses smartphones' built-in step-sensor/fitness tracker to count your steps because almost everyone has (or will have) a smartphone and carries it around all day. So there's no need to buy, wear, recharge & sync a wearable device to track your steps in StepUp.
However, we are planning to sync with HealthKit/Google Fit in the future, and you will be able to see your steps from there in StepUp - so if your wearable syncs with either, it will work with StepUp soon. Let us know which wearable you want to use with StepUp at feedback@thestepupapp.com, so we can prioritize integration accordingly.
